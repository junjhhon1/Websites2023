Common Concern

Do you offer a warranty or guarantee on your bonsai trees?

Yes, we take great pride in the quality of our bonsai trees. Each tree comes with a 30-day warranty, during which we guarantee its health and condition. If you encounter any issues within this period, please contact us, and we will be happy to assist you.

Are the bonsai trees suitable for indoor or outdoor use?

Our bonsai trees are suitable for both indoor and outdoor environments, depending on the species. Each product page provides details about the ideal placement for the specific tree. Indoor bonsai trees typically require bright light, while outdoor varieties prefer partial shade.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods, including credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and Apple Pay. Rest assured that your payment information is secure during the checkout process.

What are your shipping rates and delivery times?

Shipping rates vary based on your location and the size of your order. During the checkout process, you can view the applicable shipping charges before completing your purchase. Delivery times also depend on your location and the shipping method chosen at checkout.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship our bonsai trees internationally. Please note that international shipping rates and delivery times may vary, depending on your location. Additional customs or import duties may apply, which are the responsibility of the buyer.

What is your return policy?

We want you to be fully satisfied with your purchase. If, for any reason, you are not happy with your bonsai tree, you may return it within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange. Please review our return policy for more details.
